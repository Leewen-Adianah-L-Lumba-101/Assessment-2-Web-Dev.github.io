WHERE HOME IS

An atmospheric, dimly lit, colour heavy point-and-click styled 8-Bit game. With a dialogue driven story to tell; a harsh reflection of childhood yearning and antique memories. The game is meant as a showcase of escapism and self-inflicted punishment, where a dull unaccomplished adult life can lead one unhealthily clinging to the warm past.

All art was made with Pixilart.

Art converted into tiles for each room using Pixsy.

Music and sprites all made within the online engine Bitsy.
